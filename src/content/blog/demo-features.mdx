This post is part of the **Getting Started** series. Notice the series navigation at the top of the page.

## Music Player

The built-in music player supports:
- Playlist with shuffle/sequential modes
- Bilingual lyrics with auto-scroll
- Apple Music-style blurred cover background
- Keyboard media key control (play/pause/next/prev)
- Draggable position
- Per-article BGM override via frontmatter

Add songs in `src/data/playlist.ts`.

## Dark Mode

The theme automatically switches to dark mode after 18:00 and back to light mode at 06:00. Users can also toggle manually with the sun/moon button in the header.

## Search

Press the search icon to open full-text search. It indexes all post titles, summaries, and tags.

## Series Navigation

Add `series` and `seriesOrder` to your post frontmatter to group posts into a series. The template automatically generates previous/next navigation.

## Per-Article BGM

Add `bgm: "Song Name"` to your frontmatter to auto-switch the music player when readers open your post.

## Comments

Enable [Waline](https://waline.js.org) comments by setting `serverURL` in `src/config.ts`.

## Deployment

This template works great with:
- **Cloudflare Pages** — Free, fast, global CDN
- **Vercel** — Zero-config deployment
- **Netlify** — Simple and reliable
