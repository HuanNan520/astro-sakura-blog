Welcome to your new blog! This template is built with [Astro](https://astro.build) and styled with a cherry blossom aesthetic.

## Getting Started

1. Edit `src/config.ts` to personalize your site
2. Add your own blog posts in `src/content/blog/`
3. Configure the music player in `src/data/playlist.ts`
4. Deploy to Cloudflare Pages, Vercel, or Netlify

## Features

- **Music Player** — Built-in player with playlist, lyrics sync, and MediaSession support
- **Dark Mode** — Automatic time-based switching (dark after 18:00)
- **Search** — Full-text client-side search
- **Series** — Group related posts into series with navigation
- **Comments** — Optional Waline integration
- **RSS** — Auto-generated RSS feed

Happy blogging!
